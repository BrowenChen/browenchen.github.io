    • Music

    I have always been a lifelong musician, and at the moment am working towards solidifying my bebop jazz and harmony knowledge on guitar with my electronic music production and sound design techniques. I am a classicaly trained violinist from age 5, and have been a jazz drummer and percussionist throughout high school. I was a featured guitar soloist in 2009 with San Diego Young Artist Symphony to play "Canon Rock" , the song which inspired me to begin the guitar. Throughout high school, I accumulated over 2,000,000 video views on YouTube and 6,000+ subscribers while producing music videos. I dabble with MIDI Synth guitars as well. I've performed in UC Berkeley's Dann Zinn Advanced Jazz Combo, Big Band, and Nu Jazz Collective, alongside my fusion funk group Constant Time . I have also taught 3 semesters of an Intro to Jazz Harmony course at Berkeley, as well as private individual instruction. I am currently enrolled in ensembles at The California Jazz Conservatory, and gigging in the Bay area.

    Jazz-Fusion

    I'm heavily interested in modern fusion - a genre that combines jazz harmony and theory with modern rock and pop elements. Interested in the neuroscience of jazz improvisation?



    Electronic Production

    I aim to create more EDM, Progressive House, Pop type tracks, eventually incorporating more fusion guitar and guitar synth ideas. Currently use Logic Pro X, Omnisphere, Serum, and Massive for production and sound design.




    Events & Contact

    Feel free to reach out for any reason! Jazz trio, full band, music production collaborations, session guitar work, private instruction, full-stack web application dev inquires, or just general chit chat. I love meeting new people always enjoy good conversations over coffee.

    Code

    Here are a few samples of projects I've worked on in the past at LinkedIn, Zzish in London's Techstar's Accelerator, side projects, and Berkeley course projects. I work predominantely with full-stack web technologies (NodeJS, Ruby on Rails, BackboneJS, AngularJS, PostGreSQL, MongoDB) and am interested in machine learning (scipy, numpy) and occassional audio and music programming languages (MaxMSP). Code samples for some Machine Learning , Computer Networking , and Computer Security projects can be also found at my Github.

    Berkeley Computational Cognition Lab

    Built the "Cognitive Tools for Self Mastery" project individually as an undergraduate research assistant, led by P.h.d candidate Falk Lieder and Professor Tom Griffiths in Berkeley's Computational Cognition Lab, drawing on their published paper on the gamification of optimal decision making based on machine learning, A.I research, behavioral experiments, and mathematical modeling to understand procrastination and decision making. This Ruby on Rails full-stack web app is built for first round experiments, with it's full features, functions, and data tracking aims outlined in the design document for the semester long project.

    Tempo - Productive Time Waster

    Have free time? Want to be productive? Well here's the solution for you. Built with a team of 5 for a Berkeley software engineering semester course, Tempo allows users to optimize their free time. Pulls in various articles, videos, activities related to your personal interests that fit your specified time constraints, as well as the ability to create your own custom activities to do in the future. Built on a Rails, BackboneJS, PostGre stack, utilizing Youtube, Reddit, NYT API's, and full integration testing libraries. Create an account, try it out!

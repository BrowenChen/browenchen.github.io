Owen Chen

Jazz Guitarist

Owen Chen is a

Guitarist Owen Chen is currently based in New York City, where he is active in the New York jazz scene, and has roots in the San Francisco Bay Area scene, as well as maintaining an active musical internet presence on Instagram.

He went to New York University for his Masters in Music Degree in Jazz Instrumental Performance . He released his debut album "Eternal" in May 2022 with his quintet, comprising of original contemporary jazz compositions. As a classical violinist from the the age of 5 and with 20+ years of musical experience, Owen futhered his musical studies at NYU with world reknowned jazz musicians and leading figures in jazz guitar, having studied privately with Adam Rogers, Peter Bernstein, Alex Sipiagin, John Scofield, Ari Hoenig and more. Owen can be seen performing locally in New York City leading jazz groups in notable venues regularly such as The Red Pavilion, Laissez Faire, Bar Next Door, collaborating with NYC based organizations for private events, and performing in all styles.

Originally from San Diego, Owen studied and performed in UC Berkeley's jazz ensembles and California Jazz Conservatory and is an alumni from UC Berkeley with degrees in Computer Science & Cognitive Science. Mentors in the past have included Jeff Massanari, Dann Zinn, Steve Erquiaga, Myra Melford, and Ted Moore. In 2019, Owen was a semi-finalist for the international Wilson Center Jazz Guitar Competition. Owen began music at the age of five, studying classical violin and playing in local symphony orchestras in San Diego. Album

Debut Album (EP) "Eternal" of 4 original compositions was released on April 24th 2022. iTunes Link.

Each piece draws from different sources of personal musical inspiration in Owen's life experiences, from influences of cinematic soundtracks, progressive rock, and anime music, the contrasting themes and melodic improvisations paint a picture of the different melodies that contributed to his "eternal" musical journey.


This music was brought to life by a phenomenal band of New York musicians - Sukyung Kim (piano), Sunhyun Yoo (saxophone), Sean Hannon (Bass), and Joshua Roberts (Drums)

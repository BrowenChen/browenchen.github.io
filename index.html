Owen Chen

Jazz Guitarist

Owen Chen is a

Guitarist Owen Chen is currently based in New York City, where he is active in the New York jazz scene, and has roots in the San Francisco Bay Area scene, as well as maintaining an active musical internet presence on Instagram.

He went to New York University for his Masters Degree in Jazz Instrumental Performance and is an alumni from UC Berkeley with degrees in Computer Science & Cognitive Science. As a classical musician from the the age of 5 and with 20+ years of musical experience, Owen futhered his musical studies at NYU with world reknowned jazz musicians and leading figures in jazz guitar, having studied privately with Adam Rogers, Peter Bernstein, Alex Sipiagin, John Scofield and more. Owen can be seen performing locally in New York City with various jazz groups.

Originally from the Bay Area, Owen studied and performed in UC Berkeley's jazz ensembles and California Jazz Conservatory. Mentors in the past have included Jeff Massanari, Dann Zinn, Steve Erquiaga, Bob Ross, Myra Melford, and Ted Moore. He also attended the SIM (School for Improvisational Music) Summer Intensive at the Brooklyn Conservatory of Music. In 2019, Owen was a semi-finalist for the international Wilson Center Jazz Guitar Competition. Owen began music at the age of five, studying classical violin and playing in local symphony orchestras in San Diego. In 2009, he was featured as a guitar soloist for the San Diego Young Artists Orchestra to play “Canon Rock”, the original tune that inspired him to pick up the guitar.

For music booking inquiries, event booking for a jazz trio or quartet, lessons, or collaborations, send an email to music.owenchen@gmail.com .

Producing


Technology.

Apart from music, Owen writes code as a software engineer for LinkedIn, contributing to building software products that influence 500 million users. Owen has completed his dual Bachelors degrees in Computer Science and Cognitive Science at UC Berkeley, having conducted research in the field of Computational Cognition, published in Nature Human Behavior scientific journal, and has startup industry experience in educational technology in London startup accelerators (TechStars).
